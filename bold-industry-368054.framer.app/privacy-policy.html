PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated “30th June 2025”

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website services.We are committed to handling your data with care, transparency, and in compliance with applicable data protection laws. Whether you're browsing our website, contacting us, or subscribing to our updates, we ensures your information is treated responsibly. This policy explains the types of information's we gather, why we collect it, and the choices you have regarding your data. We use industry-standard security practices to safeguard your information against unauthorized access or misuse. Our goal is to provide a secure and trustworthy experience for every user.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when interacting with our services, such as your name, email address, phone number, and company details. This includes data submitted through contact forms, newsletter signups, account creation. Additionally we automatically gather non personal data such browser type, device information, and usage statistics using cookies and tracking tools. This helps us understand how users interact with our platform and allows us to improve functionality. We may also receive information from third-party platforms if you interact with us on social media or via integrations.

All collected data is stored securely and used solely for enhancing your experience with our website and services. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors without parental consent. Your privacy matters to us, and we are transparent about what information we collect and why.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. This includes responding to inquiries, processing requests, personalizing user experiences, and delivering updates or newsletters you’ve opted into. We may also use your data to analyze website performance, detect fraudulent activity, or ensure site security. Internally, your information supports our marketing.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and understand user behavior. Cookies are data files stored on your device that help remember your preferences, login details, and session activities. Some cookies are essential for site functionality, while others allow us to analyze site performance and personalize content.

We may also use third-party cookies from services like Google Analytics or advertising platforms to track engagement or conversions. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time, though this may affect site functionality. By using our website, you consent to our use of these technologies. We strive to ensure that all tracking tools are secure, minimal, and serve only to improve your experience. Your data is not stored in plain text and is never used to personally identify you unless explicitly stated or requested.

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your personal information and how it’s used. You may request access to the data we hold, ask for corrections, or even request deletion of your personal details. If you no longer wish to receive communications from us, you can easily unsubscribe using the link provided in our emails. Additionally, you can opt out of cookie tracking by adjusting your browser settings
